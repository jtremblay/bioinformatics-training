In this example, we have a subset of files taken from the Tremblay et al., 2019 (https://sfamjournals.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1111/1462-2920.14609) large scale shotgun MG and MT study.

KOs_hydrocarbon.txt                       # Simple short list of 5 KEGG Orthologs (KO).
genes.faa                                 # List of genes we want to query for the KOs of interest above.
merged_gene_abundance_cpm.tsv             # Normalized abundance values of the genes listed in the above genes.faa file.
